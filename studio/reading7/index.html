Can Jokes Bring Down Governments?

Memes are created to act as “political posters”. It was very new to find out that there are three qualities needed for successful memes: longevity, fecundity, and copying fidelity, because I have never thought that memes would incorporate such complex and meaningful intentions.

The way how word ‘meme’ was created was very interesting to me. In fact, the word is abbreviated from ‘Mimeme’ which came from a Greek root but Dawkins who introduced the notion of meme as a “cultural gene” wanted the word to sound like ‘gene’.
